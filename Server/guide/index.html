McRubes Media Server

Your personal Netflix — quick guide to watching and requesting content

🎬 Watching Movies & TV Shows

  • Open Plex on your TV, phone, or browser — mcrubes.local:32400/web
  • Sign in with your Plex account (create a free one at plex.tv if you don't have one)
  • Find "McRubes" under More > Your Servers to see Movies and TV Shows
  • Pick something and press play — subtitles, resume, and watchlists all work like Netflix
Tip: If a video won't play, tap ⚙ during playback and switch quality to "Original" or lower.

📥 Requesting New Movies or TV Shows

Don't see something you want? Request it — it usually shows up within an hour.

  • Go to mcrubes.local:5055 and sign in with your Plex account
  • Search for the movie or show by name
  • Hit the orange Request button — for TV shows you can pick specific seasons
  • It downloads automatically and appears in Plex when ready
Note: Watched movies and shows are automatically cleaned up after 14 days to save space. Request again anytime to rewatch.
